About Me

My research centers on the integration of Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning, Robotics, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to develop intelligent systems capable of real-time sensing, analysis, and decision-making. I explore the use of machine learning algorithms and AI models to process data collected from embedded and connected devices, enabling predictive analytics, automation, and adaptive control. My work includes applications in smart healthcare, intelligent robotics, and real-time monitoring systems. I am particularly interested in building data-driven, context-aware solutions that leverage AI and IoT to solve complex real-world problems across various domains.
